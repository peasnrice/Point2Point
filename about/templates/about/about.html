What we are all about

Point2Point was created in the spring/autumn of 2014 by Andy while he was stuck in limbo waiting for his perminant residency application to come through.

He feels for anybody else that may be in a similar situation, there is no other way to put it, it sucks, and that's that.

Andy went to London, England over the new year of 2013 for vacation and participated in something similar to what this is, HiddenCity.

The thing is, this didn't exist back home so he thought why not have a go at bringing back the idea and developing it.

So he did and he hopes that people enjoy it.
